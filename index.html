Our Services

Our machinery lineup is best-in-class, fully equipped to support all your print project requirements.

01

Design

High-precision pre-press services featuring Computer-to-Plate (CTP) technology for maximum detail and sharpness.

02

Press

Large-scale printing processes using modern offset machines for consistent, fast, and economical results.

03

Finishing

Premium final touches starting from cutting, laminating, to embossing/pond for high aesthetic value.

01

Design

Flexo design is tailored for labels, stickers, and flexible packaging, with a focus on solid colors, readability, and accurate material compatibility.

02

Press

Flexo press provides high speed on materials such as film, plastic, and kraft paper, making it suitable for large-volume production with stable and efficient printing results.

03

Finishing

Flexo finishing includes varnishing, lamination, embossing, and perforation to make products more durable, attractive, and suitable for market needs.

01

Design

Security printing design includes anti-counterfeiting elements such as holograms, watermarks, microtext, and special patterns for high-value documents.

02

Press

Security presses utilizing specialized inks and precision printing techniques to produce official documents, certificates, security labels, and tax stamps to the highest standards.

03

Finishing

Security finishing includes anti-tear lamination, protective coating, and quality inspection to ensure documents are durable and secure.
